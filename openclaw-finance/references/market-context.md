# Market Context — APEX Reference

## Macro Regime Classification

| Regime | Indicators | Strategy Adjustment |
|--------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Risk-On** | VIX < 18, BTC ↑, DXY ↓, yields stable | Full allocation, aggressive entries |
| **Risk-Off** | VIX > 25, BTC ↓, DXY ↑, yields rising | Reduce positions 50%, tighter stops |
| **Transitioning** | Mixed signals, regime uncertainty | Half position sizes, wider stops |
| **Crisis** | VIX > 35, correlation spike, liquidity drying | Cash only, hedge positions, no new entries |

## Key Correlations to Monitor
- BTC ↔ ETH: ~0.85 (highly correlated — don't overweight both)
- BTC ↔ SPY: ~0.40 (moderate — diversification benefit)
- SOL ↔ BTC: ~0.75 (high — SOL amplifies BTC moves)
- DXY ↔ BTC: ~-0.50 (inverse — strong dollar = weak crypto)
- VIX ↔ SPY: ~-0.80 (inverse — fear = sell-off)
- Gold ↔ BTC: ~0.30 (low — true diversifier)
