# Moonshot Patterns — What Makes a Token Moon

## Early Signals (First 30 minutes)
- DexScreener profile created with social links (Twitter, Telegram, website)
- Boosted on DexScreener (someone paid to promote it)
- Multiple buy transactions from different wallets (not one whale)
- Liquidity added > $10K (real commitment from dev)
- Volume > $50K in first hour

## Strong Signals (First 2 hours)
- Listed on GeckoTerminal with growing pool reserves
- Reddit/Twitter mentions increasing
- Multiple DEX pairs created (not just one)
- Holder count growing (>50 holders)
- Price holding or rising after initial buy pressure

## Red Flags (Avoid)
- Mint authority not revoked (dev can print infinite tokens)
- Freeze authority enabled (dev can lock your wallet)
- Single wallet holds >20% of supply
- Bundle/sniper activity detected
- No social media presence
- Liquidity < $5K
- Copy of existing token name/symbol

## Scoring Weights
- DexScreener profile: 20 pts
- DexScreener boosted: 25 pts
- GeckoTerminal pool: 15 pts
- Twitter mentions > 3: 15 pts
- Liquidity > $10K: 10 pts
- Volume > $50K: 10 pts
- Social links present: 5 pts
